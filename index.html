About

The Minnesota Welcome Center provides free information and services to help people from foreign countries now living in the United States find the support and resources they need to succeed in the community.

Overcome Addictions

Addiction Recovery Classes
Recovery support groups can help anyone struggling with an addiction or compulsive behavior. Support group meetings use an adapted version of the Twelve Steps of Alcoholics Anonymous that teaches addiction recovery principles from a Christian faith perspective.
Those struggling with addiction or compulsive behaviors can attend a recovery support group to receive support, help, and fellowship from other in similar circumstances as well as from group facilitators. You can attend meetings in person or over the phone. The meeting are free, participants remain anonymous to one another, and all information shared will remain confidential.
Schedule: Wednesdays, 12:00pm
This is a virtual class on Zoom.

Resources

Our volunteer staff can meet confidentially with individuals and couples to discuss needs, provide guidance, and refer immigrants to the many other free resources available within our community.

Please visit us during our regular evening hours on Tuesdays and Thursdays, 7-8:30 pm, at the Welcome Center within The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, 3921 Nicollet Ave, Minneapolis. For those unable to attend in person, please reach out to us at MNWelcomeCenter@gmail.com or by phone at (218) 675-1836 to schedule a meeting via Zoom.
